The Miracles of the Namiya General Store

Book Introduction

"The Miracles of the Namiya General Store" is a novel by Japanese author Keigo Higashino. The story revolves around a mysterious general store where people can leave letters seeking advice, and they receive unexpected responses. Through intertwining narratives, the novel explores themes of fate, human connection, and the unseen impact of kindness.
